Public Safety

AIT provides solutions for the forensic analysis of audio-video material, with features that address the requirements of end users including service providers and stakeholders such as law enforcement agencies (LEAs). Our solution supports LEAs in analysing, processing, and gaining information from large amounts of evidence data, such as video or audio recordings, pictures, and internet web pages. It deals with heterogeneity, varying quality of data acquired from a wide variety of data sources, and allows investigators to identify vehicles, weapons, sounds, sentences, and key words, and link them to relevant events. Human operators are involved at many points along the different investigation phases, beginning with the data acquisition and selection, over the information extraction, to the assessment and decision support stage.
